EASY MODE
1. In easy mode you will have 10 seconds to memorize the deck cards.
2. After 10 seconds all cards will be dealt face down and the game will begin.
3. You will have 30 seconds to find all pairs. Each attempt, right or wrong, will be counted and will set your final score.
4. The game ends after the player matches all pairs or the time is up.
